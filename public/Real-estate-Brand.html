Belle Monde - Real Estate Business 

Website for a Real Estate company. The purpose of this website is to showcase their various projects and establish a credible Brand.

Interaction Design | Research | Prototyping | Branding | UX  

Problem Statement


Designing a website for a Real Estate company. The purpose of this website is to showcase their various projects. It can be ongoing projects, completed projects etc...Each project will have an individual landing page connected to the website. The website has to be planned in such a way that it is best compatible for SEO activities.

Brand Idea

Modernism would be described as one of the most optimistic styles In architecture. There is simplicity in modern Interior design that has given it everlasting relevance. Modern homes have clean lines and are without fuss yet they can be comfortable. Our Brand would portray the modernism that has been described. Our brand would reflect the minimalism, the positivity and the optimism. Provide credibility to the brand is the main Idea. Being an credible brand helps the brand to grow and also helps the user when it comes to choosing our brand not only In terms of property but it also helps In legal matter and bank loans, as banks are more secured when giving loan to the buyer.

Ecosystem

The ecosystem for a real estate company could consist of user from different ecosystems. When talking about buying a flat In a residential area, we can find a few different kinds of users:

A person who wants to buy a house for living.
A person who is buying a house as an investment He wants the house and area to give him maximum returns in terms of per sq. ft and also rent.

Although being a luxury brand they provide homes for every type of customer. Whether 1BHK to 3BHK to commercial space for small or big company . When we talk about commercial area although much more expensive in terms of land and rent. But companies want as much facility in terms of transport, security office essential facility and convenience. 

Approach

Users often perceive aesthetically pleasing design as design that's more usable.
(Aesthetic Usability Effect) With the help of this law we would create a simple yet aesthetically pleasing website with all the necessary Information about each real-estate project. Users spend most of their time on other sites. This means that users prefer your site to work the same way as all the other sites they already know. (Jacobs Law) Using some common features of other competitors we would improve on them and make our site more interactive. Elements tend to be perceived into groups If they are sharing an area with a clearly defined boundary(Law of common region). Objects that are near 0r are in close proximity to each other are tend to be grouped together(Law of Proximity).
