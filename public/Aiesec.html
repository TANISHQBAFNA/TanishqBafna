Brief History of AIESEC 


AIESEC, is a global, non – political, independent, not-for-profit organisation run by students and recent graduates of higher education, present in over 126 countries and territories and with over
80,000 members. Its members are interested in world issues, leadership and management. AIESEC provides its members with an integrated development experience comprised of leadership opportunities, international internships and participation in a global learning environment.
